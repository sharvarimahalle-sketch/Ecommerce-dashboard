# E-commerce Customer Behaviour Analysis

# Project Title
E-commerce Customer Behaviour Analysis Dashboard

# Description / Purpose
This project focuses on analyzing customer purchasing behavior in an e-commerce environment to uncover patterns in revenue generation, customer segmentation and engagement.
The dashboard helps stakeholders understand who their customers are, how they shop and what drives revenue, enabling data driven decision making.

# Tech Stack
Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
DAX – KPI calculations and measures
Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning

# Data Source
Structured dataset in CSV format containing:
  Customer demographics (Age, Gender, City)
  Transaction details (Revenue, Purchases, Discounts)
  Customer behavior metrics (Last purchase, Spend patterns)

# Features / Highlights
• Business Problem
E-commerce businesses often struggle to understand:
- Which customers contribute most to revenue
- How discounts impact sales
- Which demographics drive higher engagement
- Where to focus marketing efforts
  
• Goal of the Dashboard
- Analyze customer purchase behavior
- Identify high-value customer segments
- Evaluate the effectiveness of discounts
- Track revenue trends across demographics and locations

# Walkthrough of Key Visuals
• Top KPI Cards - Total Revenue, Total Items Purchased, Customer Count, Avg Spend per Customer, Avg Days Since Last Purchase: Retention signal
• Revenue Breakdown (Donut Chart) - Shows contribution by membership tiers (Gold, Silver, Bronze), helping identify premium customers.
• Sales Split by Gender (Bar Chart) - Highlights purchasing differences between male and female customers.
• Age-wise Revenue Analysis (Line Chart) - Identifies which age groups contribute most to revenue.
• Sales Impact of Discounts (Bar Chart) - Evaluates whether discounts are effectively increasing revenue.
• Satisfaction Level Analysis- Measures customer satisfaction distribution to assess experience quality.
• Revenue Contribution by City (Bar Chart) - Identifies top-performing cities for targeted marketing.
• City wise Customer Ratings - Compares satisfaction across locations.
• Interactive Filters (Slicers) - Gender, Membership Type, Discount usage, City

# Business Impact 
• Identified high revenue customer segments based on demographics
• Revealed top-performing cities, enabling focused regional strategies
• Showed how discounts influence purchasing behavior, helping optimize pricing strategy
• Highlighted customer retention patterns using recency metrics
• Enabled stakeholders to make data-driven marketing and sales decisions
